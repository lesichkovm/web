Image Utilities

Efficiently load background images to avoid "flash of unstyled content" or blank backgrounds while images are downloading.

$$.loadBackgroundImages()

Finds all elements with the data-background-image attribute and applies them as background-image styles only after the image has been fully pre-loaded by the browser.

<div class="hero" data-background-image="/images/large-hero.jpg">...</div>

<script>
  $$.loadBackgroundImages();
</script>
